# Design

The Design section provides tools to customize the visual appearance of your J2Commerce store, from the layout of product pages to the professional look of your invoices and emails.

## Customizing Layouts

If you want to change how J2Commerce displays content on your site, use [Template Overrides](../design/template-overrides.md). This allows you to modify the PHP and HTML without losing your changes during updates.

## Customer Communications

Control the professional touchpoints of your store through:
- [Email Templates](../design/email-templates.md) — Customize the automated emails sent to customers.
- [Invoice Templates](../design/invoice-templates.md) — Design the invoices, receipts, and packing slips sent with orders.

## Design Best Practices

- **Use Overrides:** Never edit the core J2Commerce files. Always use the Overrides manager.
- **Test Printing:** Always print a test copy of your Invoice templates to ensure they fit on a standard A4/Letter page.
- **Mobile First:** When creating layout overrides, ensure they remain responsive using Bootstrap 5 classes.
